PRIVACY POLICY - "Medieval warrior"


This page discloses application access, collection, use, and sharing of the data. 



Information collection and use

Starting from application release date(01 March 2021) the only data stored locally on your device is related to game progress and player's equipment configuration and purchases. This data is stored in private application directory and no other app can access it because of protection provided by Android OS.

No data is sent to any server or third parties.


Upcoming planned changes

All changes will be notified to user on their implementation date and will allow to refuse or restrict further usage of application if user don't wish to comply with changes.

In the future it is planned to add online multiplayer mode, Ad services and in-app purchases which will require to change this policy and collect minimum required data to provide better experience for users(such as logged-in user email to allow bans for cheating, optionally allow identification for online back-up data on user demand, exchange players nicknames, determine pings to server, etc.). In case of Ad services and in-app purchases it may require to store and provide on demand cookies or other identification form which required by related services.

All data share required will be restricted to described purpose and will not be sold or provided to any other parties. To provide security sensitive private data will be exchanged by HTTPS protocol with built-in encryption and additionally all sensitive user data will be encrypted if possible.





This policy is effective starting from 19 January 2022.
